Welcome to Klondike Solitaire!

The goal of the game is simple. In order to win, you must
move all the cards to the top 4 stacks. Each stack can only
have one suit attributed to it, and cards must be arranged
in ascending order (Ace to King).

Below the 4 empty stacks, you will find 7 tableau stacks.
These can be used to arrange cards to move up later. Cards
in these stacks must be arranged in descending order (King to Ace)
and must alternate suit color i.e. to place a card on a black 7,
the card must be a red 6.

Another way to reveal cards is through the stockpile in the top
left corner. By default, when clicked, the pile will flip 3 cards
with only the top card being playable. If you would like an easier
option, you may choose to change the game to "Turn One" in the
settings menu.

There is also an undo button in case you decide to try a different
move.

Have fun!
